    Eiffel Tower

    Europe

    The Eiffel Tower, an iconic symbol of Paris, rises to 324 meters and offers breathtaking views of the city. Built for the Exposition Universelle in 1889, it is a masterpiece of wrought iron designed by Gustave Eiffel. Today, it attracts millions of visitors every year, making it one of the most visited monuments in the world.

    Taj Mahal

    Asia

    The Taj Mahal in Agra, India is a magnificent white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Completed in 1653, it is an example of magnificent Mughal architecture and craftsmanship. This symbol of eternal love attracts millions of visitors every year.
